# 🧵 Textile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Textile Sales Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer trends, regional distribution, payment methods, and product categories.

The dashboard helps convert raw textile sales data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track quantity sold and order volume
- Analyze sales across regions and categories
- Identify top customers and salespersons
- Understand payment method distribution
- Compare textile product performance

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales by Region
Analyzes revenue contribution across:
- Asia
- North America
- Europe

### 2. Quantity by Textile Type
Tracks product quantity sold:
- Woolen Fabric
- Cotton Fabric
- Silk Fabric
- Denim

### 3. Sales Trend Over Time
Monthly sales comparison:
- August
- September

### 4. Payment Method Distribution
Breakdown of sales by:
- Bank Transfer
- PayPal
- Credit Card
- Cash

### 5. Top Customers Analysis
Identifies highest contributing customers.

### 6. Sales by Salesperson
Compares individual sales performance.

### 7. Category Distribution
Sales comparison:
- Blended Fabric
- Natural Fibers

### 8. Interactive Filters
Dashboard includes slicers for:
- Region
- Category
- Textile Type
- Payment Method
- Order Date
- Salesperson
- Customer

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- Asia generated the highest sales contribution.
- Woolen Fabric recorded the highest quantity sold.
- September showed significant sales growth.
- Bank Transfer was the most preferred payment method.
- Top customers contributed a major portion of revenue.
